About

My Story

I am currently based in Lake Forest, CA.

When I am not working, I love spending time with my husband. I enjoy going on hikes; visiting breweries to find the next best sour; playing board games; playing videogames (League of Legends); cooking; baking; drawing; and crafting (such as making felt ornaments and cosplaying).

A fun fact, when I was a kid, I aspired to be a voice actor. Since childhood, I have always loved making funny voices, noises, and imitating characters on TV. I would generally consider myself to be shy in demeanor when first meeting someone, but once I have warmed up to you, you might even hear a different voice slip out.

For years, I was stumped by the age-old question: What do you want to be when you grow up? Originally, I had wanted to pursue the arts, hence, why I have an Associate in Arts in Fine and Applied Arts. After years of searching, I stumbled upon UI Design. Intrigued by the blending of art, technology, and user focus, like Detective Pikachu, I thought to myself, Oh you feel it. You feel it in your jellies, don't you? I then knew, that UI/UX Design was the career path for me. Through CareerFoundry, I was able to pursue the change of my career goals. With the completion of the Introduction to UI Design and UI Immersion courses. I can now call myself a UI Designer. I am looking for opportunities to grow both my designer mindset and skillset. I have always had a knack for the details in things and hope to bring this, among many other tools, to the table.
